About the workbench

Practical calculations for print-production decisions.

Print Production Lab turns everyday prepress, stock, binding and estimating relationships into transparent, editable tools.

What this site does

It helps estimators, designers, self-publishers and production operators make a calculation visible before a job is released. Each tool states its unit, method and limitation.

Who it is for

Commercial printers, prepress teams, book designers, sign shops and small brands can use the workbench to prepare a clearer specification.

How calculations are built

Methods use direct geometry, quantity or weight relationships. Inputs remain editable because stock, press and finishing requirements vary.

Editorial approach

We revise calculators when a clear production issue is identified. Results are planning aids, not press-ready certification.
