Wide-Format Resolution Calculator

Calculate input PPI at a final print width, then assess it alongside your viewing distance and production workflow.

Enter values.

Method

PPI equals image pixels divided by final width in inches. For example, 6,000 pixels over 60 inches is 100 PPI. Viewing distance is recorded for context, not used as a universal quality guarantee.

Assumptions and limitations

Perceived quality depends on content, RIP scaling, substrate, ink set, screen, lighting and viewing distance. Confirm target resolution with the print provider.
