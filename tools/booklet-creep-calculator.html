Booklet Creep Calculator

Estimate the maximum outward creep of a saddle-stitched booklet. Use measured sheet caliper in mm.

Enter values, then calculate.

Calculation method

Maximum creep = (page count ÷ 4 − 1) × sheet caliper. A 64-page booklet on 0.10 mm stock has 16 sheets and an estimated 1.50 mm maximum creep.

Assumptions and limitations

This estimate assumes saddle stitching and uniform stock. Folding, compression, trimming, grain direction and press workflow can change the final compensation.
