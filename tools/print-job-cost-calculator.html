Print Job Cost Calculator

Start with your own material, setup, labor and finishing figures. No default value is a market price.

Enter your job values.

Method

Total cost = editable cost components. Cost per finished piece = total cost ÷ finished quantity. Add spoilage to material quantities before entering material cost, and apply a desired margin after calculating cost.

Assumptions and limitations

This is an estimating aid, not a quote. Tax, freight, spoilage, overhead allocation, press downtime and target margin remain user-controlled assumptions.
